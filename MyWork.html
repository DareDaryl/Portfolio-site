ABOUT ME

I love coding!

I love coding! I never knew I would and had always been to afraid to learn it. I had thought the world of software engineering was available only to the geniuses and intellectual titans of the world. I had believed that the complexities of code would be too much for the average person to comprehend. One of the things that I've come to realize throughout my career and personal life, is that EVERYTHING is accessible if you give it enough time, attention and training. I had reached a stalemate in my career as an Operations Manager and really wanted something that would challenge the creative part of my brain. While I was going through this existential career crisis, I started googling classes for engineering and ended up reading about some very comprehensive and fun Software Bootcamps. I spent a month curating the one that would work best for me and ended up doing a 6 month long, 40 hours a week full stack coding course. I haven't looked back. 

Daryl Shifflett
